Revolutionizing Agro-Business for Sustainable Livelihoods

At Casina Farms, we are dedicated to creating a sustainable future through innovative agro-business practices and landscape restoration. By partnering with local coastal communities in Kenya, we aim to transform smallholder farming approaches, ensuring sustainable livelihoods and environmental conservation. Our holistic support spans capacity building, resource mobilization, business administration, and market reach, fostering resilient and prosperous communities.

Our Vision

To lead a global movement where smallholder coastal farming communities thrive, supported by healthy ecosystems, prosperous farmers, vibrant local economies, and sustainable practices that guarantee food security for generations to come.


To lead a transformative movement in smallholder coastal farming that integrates sustainable practices, empowers local communities, and restores vital ecosystems, fostering lasting positive impacts through collaboration between communities and landscapes.

Our Main Focus

  • Mangrove Honey Value Chain

    Casina Farms has partnered with coastal communities living adjacent to mangrove ecosystems to explore and revolutionize the mangrove honey value chain. Our efforts focus on active mangrove restoration and conservation, providing a unique and sustainable source of income for local communities.

    Why Mangrove Honey?

    • Sustainable Livelihoods: By purchasing our mangrove honey, you support the marginalized coastal communities we work with, helping them achieve economic stability and resilience.
    • Environmental Conservation: Your purchase contributes directly to the restoration and conservation of Kenya's threatened mangrove ecosystems, playing a crucial role in combating climate change and protecting biodiversity.

  • Innovative Climate-Smart Agriculture

    Casina Farms Mkulima is another transformative project by Casina Farms. We partner with smallholder farmers to enhance food security and promote climate justice through innovative climate-smart agricultural practices.

    How We Support Smallholder Farmers:

    • Capacity Building: Providing training and resources to adopt sustainable farming methods.
    • Resource Mobilization: Assisting farmers in accessing necessary inputs and technologies.
    • Business Administration: Offering guidance on effective farm management and business strategies.
    • Market Reach: Connecting farmers to broader markets, ensuring fair prices and increased profitability.
